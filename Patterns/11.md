### Print the following pattern

Assume N is always odd.
Note : There is space after every star.

Pattern for N = 5
```
    *
   * *
  *   *
 *     *
*       *
 *     *
  *   *
   * *
    *
```
**Input Format :**

N (Total no. of rows)

**Output Format :**

Pattern in N lines

**Sample Input :**

```7```

**Sample Output :**

```
      *
     * *
    *   *
   *     *
  *       *
 *         *
  *       *
   *    *
    *  *
      *
```
